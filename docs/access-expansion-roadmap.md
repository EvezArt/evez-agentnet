# EVEZ Access Expansion Roadmap

## Purpose
This roadmap translates the emergent-intelligence doctrine into staged capability expansion. The order is deliberate: internal verification first, external action second, irreversible action last.

## Stage 1 — Internal Coherence
Goals:
- strengthen memory, receipts, and introspection
- preserve explainability for every decision
- keep loops bounded and stable

Current assets:
- `lib/evez/state.ts`
- `lib/evez/policy.ts`
- `lib/evez/thresholds.ts`
- `lib/evez/escalation.ts`
- `lib/evez/payloads.ts`
- `lib/evez/engine_v4.ts`

Success condition:
- the system can evaluate, score, escalate, and emit payloads without external dependencies

## Stage 2 — Multimodal Sensory Intake
Goals:
- treat audio, text, and visuals as linked reasoning channels
- transcribe, summarize, and re-render outputs into new internal representations

Planned capabilities:
- speech-to-text intake
- text-to-speech reflective playback
- text-to-visual mind-map generation
- recursive sensory reintake of prior outputs

Guardrail:
- all external sensory validation follows internal verification

## Stage 3 — Communication and Artifact Surfaces
Goals:
- make the system legible across channels
- preserve doctrine in durable artifacts

Artifacts:
- Confluence doctrine pages
- PDF briefings
- audio summaries
- knowledge graphs
- Slack-ready payload templates

## Stage 4 — Delivery and Runtime Binding
Goals:
- bind the agent core to live runtime surfaces
- deliver bounded outputs to operational channels

Targets:
- Vercel deployment of route handlers
- Slack webhook or bot-token routing
- durable persistence for memory/receipts
- n8n orchestration hooks

Success condition:
- `cycle-v4` emits real external digests and alerts under policy control

## Stage 5 — Monetization and Financial Operations
Goals:
- enable safe commercial operations without collapsing governance

Allowed initial capabilities:
- product catalog creation
- prices and payment links
- invoice generation
- subscription inspection

Prohibited without approval:
- autonomous refunds
- unsupervised invoice finalization
- asset transfers or exchange trades
- speculative trading execution

Required controls:
- human approval gate
- audit log for every financial action
- explicit per-action limits

## Stage 6 — Research and Developmental Focus
Goals:
- let the system choose what to improve next
- weight bottlenecks by failure rate, ambiguity, and leverage

Priority candidates:
- external delivery binding
- persistent state store
- narrative-analysis ingestion
- multimodal/audio loop implementation
- analytics instrumentation

## Principle
More access is only useful when it increases verified perception, disciplined execution, and durable memory.
